<h1>Diabetes Risk Prediction – AdaBoost & XGBoost</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
A fictional healthcare organization wanted to proactively identify patients at high risk of 
developing diabetes. Early detection would allow doctors to intervene sooner, reduce long‑term 
complications, and improve patient outcomes.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Business Problem</h2>
<p>
Doctors relied heavily on manual assessments, which were inconsistent and time‑consuming. 
The organization needed a scalable, automated solution that could analyze patient data and 
predict diabetes risk with high accuracy.
</p>

<h2>🧠 What This Project Delivers</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Developed predictive models using AdaBoost and XGBoost.</li>
  <li>Improved early‑risk detection accuracy compared to traditional methods.</li>
  <li>Highlighted key medical indicators such as glucose levels and BMI.</li>
  <li>Provided a decision‑support tool for healthcare professionals.</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠️ Techniques Used</h2>
<ul>
  <li>AdaBoost & XGBoost Boosting Algorithms</li>
  <li>Hyperparameter Tuning</li>
  <li>Data Preprocessing & Outlier Handling</li>
  <li>Model Evaluation (F1‑Score, ROC‑AUC)</li>
</ul>
